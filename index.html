Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long will the detailing service take?

+

Service times vary by package: Essential Detail (2-3 hours), Complete Detail (4-6 hours), Ultimate Protection (6-8 hours). We recommend scheduling in advance to ensure availability.

What types of cars do you service?

+

We service all vehicle types including sedans, SUVs, luxury imports, sports cars, and vintage vehicles. Our team is experienced with high-end brands like Mercedes, BMW, Audi, Porsche, and more.

How often should I get my car detailed?

+

For optimal protection and appearance, we recommend professional detailing every 3-4 months. Ceramic-coated vehicles can go 6-12 months between major details with regular maintenance.

Do you offer mobile service or pick-up?

+

Yes! We offer mobile detailing services for basic packages. For complete and ultimate packages, we provide pick-up and drop-off services within Lahore for an additional fee.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the service?

+

Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee - if you're not completely happy with any aspect of our service, we'll re-do it at no extra charge until you're satisfied.
